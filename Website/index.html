When they learn to read, they can love learning

When your child can’t decode the Hebrew text, they can’t understand it. And when they can’t understand, they become frustrated, angry, and embarrassed.

Your child doesn’t need to struggle with Chumash, Gemara, or parsha anymore. They don’t need to act out because they can’t keep up. They don’t need to feel ashamed of their inability to read.

I’m Chani Karp, Ms. ED, and I use a specialized system — crafted from years of research and training — to:

Isolate your child’s reading challenges, whether auditory processing, tracking, focus, receptive language, or other issues​

Create personalized goals that match their needs and abilities

Work with your child until they can confidently read Hebrew on their own
